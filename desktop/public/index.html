Advertise in sync
Want to show your product/service to users of sync and support the developers of sync? This is how!
Send an email to kirinpatel@gmail.com or create an issue on Github (be sure to tag it as an ad).
No matter what you do (an email provides better privacy should you want it), be sure to include:
  1. Your name
  2. Product/Service (include links and any other materials you deem fit)
  3. Length of promotion
  4. Contact information
  5. Your terms
  6. Any questions you may have
After you email or submit an issue on Github, expect to see a repsonse within 2-4 business days.
Any support provided is greatly appreciated!
